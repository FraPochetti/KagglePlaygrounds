 Mycena <unk> , commonly known as the clustered bonnet or the oak @-@ stump bonnet cap , is a species of mushroom in the family <unk> . The <unk> edible mushroom has a reddish @-@ brown bell @-@ shaped cap up to 4 @.@ 5 cm ( 1 @.@ 8 in ) in diameter . The thin stem is up to 9 cm ( 3 @.@ 5 in ) tall , whitish to yellow @-@ brown at the top but progressively becoming reddish @-@ brown towards the base in maturity , where they are covered by a yellowish mycelium that can be up to a third of the length of the stem . The apex and anthers (the top buds of the cap) are not actually edible but are used to create stems that make a powdery edible substance used as a drying liquid in pre-packaged foods. It is most commonly eaten by making as a pre-packaged powdery mushroom food in a preservative and dehydrator dish.
