 
 = England national rugby union team = 
 
 The England national rugby union team represents England in rugby union . They compete in the annual Six Nations Championship with France , Ireland , Scotland , Italy , and Wales . They have won this championship on a total of 27 occasions , 13 times winning the Grand Slam , making them the most successful team in the tournament 's history . The England rugby union team are the flagship club for many teams in England. They have numerous members of professional rugby teams and have won at least six of the seven Rugby World Cup matches in the last 15 years. However, their reputation comes mainly from their success at the Super Rugby level. England has won the three major European Rugby Championship matches at the RWC in Paris as well as the U20 World Cup in 2015. In the 2016 Rugby World Cup, England lost their semifinal against Scotland, but made it through to the final in Italy with the help of an 80-0 defeat by Scotland.
