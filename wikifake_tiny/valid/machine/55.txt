It is the second film of the Ghost in the Shell series. The film was directed by Ghost in the Shell director Mamoru Oshii and produced by Ullman Johnson. Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex was released in the United States on February 7, 2007. It was released in the United Kingdom on October 19, 2007. In 2010, Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex was released in Japan on DVD and Blu-ray Disc on October 25, 2010. The first two episodes were released on July 24, 2011 in Japan.
