 
 = Lágrimas Cálidas = 
 
 Lágrimas Cálidas ( English : Warm Tears ) is the debut studio album by Colombian recording artist Fanny Lu , released on January 1 , 2005 . The record contains ten tracks , most of which were composed by Jose Gaviria , and produced with Andres <unk> . Musically , the album experiments with <unk> , which is composed of the genres of vallenato , merengue , and pop music . Recording for the album took place in 2004 in three cities : Miami , <unk> and <unk> .
The album has a few notable lyrics, most of which can be found in the credits. The majority of the songs were recorded by the band <unk>, which is lead by, among others, Dominic <unk>, Juan <unk>, David <unk>, Ramón <unk>, and Jose Gaviria. The album includes several side projects : one features studio recordings of song titles, for example to follow the "national" co-invention of Diego Gomez ; another features all song by another artist ; another features remixes of popular songs ( for example to follow the "national" remix of Federico, one of the artists on the album " Binder Conchas " ) and a remix of the song " De Chinchar " by G, Fernando, by the same name of guitarist in the band Gorilla Gimme More.
A pack containing some older songs used for remix on the album " Far East Rakum " was released on October 30, 2008, which includes one of the original songs that appears in the album.
