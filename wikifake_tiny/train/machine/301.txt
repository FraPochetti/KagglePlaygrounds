 
 = Who Am I ( Casting Crowns song ) = 
 
 " Who Am I " is a song recorded by Christian rock band Casting Crowns . Written by Mark Hall and produced by Mark A. Miller and Steven Curtis Chapman , it was released on February 22 , 2004 , as the second single from the band 's 2003 self @-@ titled debut album . A pop rock and adult contemporary ballad , the song is based around the piano and utilizes orchestral sounds . The musicians who wrote and recorded the song included guitarist Michael Belove, keyboardist Michael Vallotton, bassist Matt Macdonald, and vocalist Jason Taplin. The song has a longer initial time signature than other songs on the album and has been a popular vehicle for lyrical and musical variety.
