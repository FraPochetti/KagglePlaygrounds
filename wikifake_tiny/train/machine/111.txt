 Robert Bernard " Robbie " Fowler ( born 9 April 1975 ) is an English former professional footballer and manager who played as a striker from 1993 to 2012 . Fowler was known for being a natural scorer with an <unk> goal @-@ poaching ability . 
 Fowler is best remembered for his time at Liverpool and is the sixth @-@ highest goalscorer in the history of the Premier League . He scored 183 goals in total for Liverpool , 128 of which were scored in the Premier League ( 162 Premier League goals in total ) . He is the 10th player to score more goals in the Premier League than first team players. 
Bob Zampa (1964-2013) 
Bob Zampa, born on 5 July 1964 in Hradec Kralove (Hr. Poland), is a former Croatian professional footballer. Zampa played for national side Istria from 1963 to 1967. He is the 16th player to score more goals than first team players, and is the highest ever in the history of the English Premier League ( highest goalscorer in English Football since 1st MLS league season). In total, he scored 146 goals for Hradec Kralove (Hr. Poland), 21 of which were in the league. He is the first Croatian to score more than 100 goals in the English Premier League. 
Zampa spent the last half of his career with Belgian side PSV Eindhoven from 2003 to 2007, scoring 62 goals in 119 games (including 3 on loan) and made 53 appearances for European giants Everton from 2009 to 2011.
