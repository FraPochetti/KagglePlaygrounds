 
 = The Actor 's Children = 
 
 The Actor 's Children is a 1910 American silent short drama written by Lloyd Lonergan and produced by the Thanhouser Company in New Rochelle , New York . The film features Orilla Smith , Yale Boss , Frank Hall Crane and Nicholas Jordan . The production was not the first film subject by the company , but it was the first to be released . Both Barry O 'Neil and Lloyd B. O'Neil's New York Times articles on the film appeared on January 14, 1910. On January 27, the same day of the film's release, O 'Neil and O'Neil's Times article was reprinted on page 2.
