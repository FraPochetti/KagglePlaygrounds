 
 = Butterfly World Tour = 
 
 The Butterfly World Tour was the third concert tour by American singer @-@ songwriter Mariah Carey . The tour promoted Carey 's album at the time , Butterfly ( 1997 ) , and included songs from several of her previous albums . The tour visited Asia , Australia and the United States , with rehearsals taking place in December 1997 . Starting on January 11 , 1998 the tour spanned five shows in Asia , six in Australia , and one in Hawaii , US . On February 7, 1998 the tour ended in Hawaii, US. The original lineup included: Beatrice Blackburn (c), Jack DeJohnette, Mariah Carey, Maria Konnikova, Susan McLagan, Albert King, James Thompson, Ian Bevis, Eric Tan, Jared Tempted and Michael Pena. As of September 2006, the tour ended with the last show in Hawaii, US.
