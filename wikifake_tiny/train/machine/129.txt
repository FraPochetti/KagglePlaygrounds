 
 = Rio de Janeiro bid for the 2016 Summer Olympics = 
 
 The Rio de Janeiro bid for the 2016 Summer Olympics and Paralympics was a successful bid to host the Games of the <unk> <unk> and the XV Paralympic Games , respectively . It was submitted on September 7 , 2007 , and recognized as an Applicant city by the International Olympic Committee ( IOC ) one week after . On June 4 , 2008 , the IOC Executive Board shortlisted Rio de Janeiro with three of the six other Applicant cities — Chicago , Madrid and Tokyo ; over Baku , Doha and Prague — becoming a Candidate city during the 2008 SportAccord Convention in Athens , Greece . The IOC also recommended that Rio de Janeiro be awarded the 2020 Olympic Games. On November 23, 2009, Rio de Janeiro was awarded the 2024 Olympic Games by the IOC.
